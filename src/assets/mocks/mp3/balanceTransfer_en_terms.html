Balance Transfer

  • When you make a credit card balance transfer, you’re moving an outstanding balance from a non-BMO credit card to your BMO credit card. You could consolidate your debt and save on interest payments if you transfer a balance from other credit cards that charge higher interest to your BMO credit card with this promotional interest rate!
  • Balance transfers are treated as cash advances and the promotional interest rate applies from the date the transfer amounts are posted to your BMO credit card account (the “Account”). Any balance transfer requests received and posted to your Account after ##RELEVANT_DATE##, will not be eligible for this offer.
  • Balance transfers cannot be used to make a payment on any other BMO credit card account or any other BMO lending account or for pre-authorized payments.

Promotional Interest Rate

  • Each balance transfer amount is eligible for the promotional interest rate of ##RATE##% until the promotional rate expiry date, provided that each balance transfer is posted to your Account by ##RELEVANT_DATE##, and your Account remains in good standing. The promotional interest rate expiry date is calculated based on the first balance transfer transaction date within the offer period and any subsequent balance transfer transaction that was accepted within the same offer period will have the same expiry date. After the promotional interest rate expires, the standard rate goes into effect after the following statement cycle date.
  • This offer may be changed or withdrawn without notice and is only valid for the Account referenced in the attached offer and is not transferable.
  • If you do not make your minimum payment by the payment due date and have not paid it by the date we prepare your next statement two times in any 12-month period, you will lose your promotional interest rate and it will increase to the default rate disclosed on the card carrier or any other terms or notices we provide to you.
  • After the promotional rate expires, any remaining balance transfer amounts will be charged interest at the standard annual interest rate for cash advances, starting from the first day of the following statement period. The promotional interest rate of ##RATE##% does not apply to purchases, fees, cash advances and other charges posted to your Account during the promotional period. Please refer to your monthly statement for the interest rate(s) applicable to your Account.
  • If your Account has balances with different interest rates, such as purchases with a standard interest rate and balance transfers with a promotional interest rate, any payment that exceeds the minimum payment will be allocated proportionately to those balances according to the terms set out in the BMO Credit Card Cardholder Agreement. For example, if you have a balance of $800 in purchases at the standard interest rate and a $200 balance transfer balance at a promotional interest rate, 80 percent of your payment will be allocated to your purchases balance and 20 percent will be allocated to your balance transfer amount balance.

Balance Transfer Fee

  • Each balance transfer is subject to a fee of ##RESERVED_7##% which will be charged to your Account on the date the balance transfer is posted to your Account. For example, if an amount of $1,000 is transferred to your Account, you will be charged a fee of $##RESERVED_4##.
  • You may request a balance transfer for as low as $100, as long as the transfer amount plus the fee does not exceed the unused portion of the credit limit on your Account at the time of processing. Balance transfers are only available for balances in Canadian dollars.

Payments

  • You will pay interest on purchases unless you pay your full Account balance by the payment due date. For example, if you complete a balance transfer for $5,000 and spend $2,000 in purchases during the same statement period, you must pay the entire balance of $7,000 before the payment due date to avoid paying interest on your purchases. You will be charged interest if you only pay the amount owing for your purchases. There is no interest-free grace period for cash advances or balance transfers.

Rewards

  • Balance transfers do not qualify to earn loyalty rewards such as AIR MILES®† Reward Miles, BMO CashBack® rewards or BMO Rewards® points.
